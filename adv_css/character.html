Character Details

Discover the powers, abilities, and personality of the superhero.

Superman

Real Name: Clark Kent Powers: Super strength, super speed, invulnerability, freezing breath, flight, heat vision Weakness: Kryptonite, magic Why He's Iconic: The first superhero, a symbol of hope and justice.

Wonder Woman

Real Name: Diana Prince Powers: Super strength, speed, flight, indestructible bracelets, Lasso of Truth Weakness: Can be overpowered by extreme magic or divine beings Why She's Iconic: The ultimate warrior-princess, combining strength with compassion.

Batman

Real Name: Bruce Wayne Powers: Genius-level intellect, martial arts skills, detective skills, high-tech gadgets Weakness: Mortality, no superpowers Why He's Iconic: The Dark Knight, a brooding vigilante who fights crime with his wits and wealth.

Spiderman

Real Name: Peter Parker Powers: Wall-crawling, super strength, spider-sense, web-slinging Weakness: Mortality, self-doubt Why He's Iconic: The friendly neighborhood hero, balancing teenage angst with superhero responsibilities.

Ironman

Real Name: Tony Stark Powers: Genius-level intellect, high-tech armor, flight, repulsor rays Weakness: Mortality, dependence on technology Why He's Iconic: The armored Avenger, a billionaire playboy who uses his wealth for good.

Captain America

Real Name: Steve Rogers Powers: Super soldier serum, peak human strength, agility, shield combat Weakness: Mortality, outdated ideals Why He's Iconic: The living symbol of American patriotism, a hero out of time.

Thor

Real Name: Thor Odinson Powers: Super strength, weather manipulation, Mjolnir hammer Weakness: Mortality, arrogance Why He's Iconic: The god of thunder, a mythological hero with a cosmic destiny.

Black Panther

Real Name: T'Challa Powers: Enhanced strength, speed, agility, Vibranium suit Weakness: Mortality, isolationism Why He's Iconic: The king of Wakanda, a warrior-king who protects his people and his nation.
